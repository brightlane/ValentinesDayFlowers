Care tips for your Valentine's bouquet

To make your flowers last as long as possible: recut stems at a 45-degree angle when you get them home, use lukewarm water for roses, change the water every two days, and keep the arrangement away from ripening fruit — apples and bananas release ethylene gas which causes flowers to wilt faster.
